Stovetop Mac and Cheese

Description

Homemade creamy, cheesy macaroni and cheese...without the box! Macaroni and Cheese is a super simple, quick side or main event. Try out this yummy version of a classic comfort dish. So easy, even my 8 year old can make it!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (16 oz)
  • Grated cheddar cheese (2 cups)
  • Milk (1.5 cups)
  • Butter (1 Tbsp)
  • Flour (2 Tbsp)
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta until soft. Set aside.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan. Add flour then milk. Stir until mixture begins to thicken.
  3. Add cheese and stir until melted into sauce.
  4. Season sauce to taste.
  5. Add in pasta and stir until well coated.
