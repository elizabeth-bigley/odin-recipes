Hamburgers

Description

No need for fast food! A hamburger is a quick, easy meal to prepare at home!

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs ground beef or ground venison
  • 1 pkg Jumbo hamburger buns
  • Vegetables of choice
  • Sliced cheese if desired
  • Preferred condiments

Steps:

  1. Form meat into patties.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, garlic, and paprika
  3. Arrange patties on a large baking sheet and place in freezer while prepping the grill.
  4. Light up the grill.
  5. When the grill is ready, grill hamburger patties until juices run clear.
  6. Toast buns, and add condiments and veggies.
