Homemade Hot Chocolate Mix

Description

Pre-packaged hot chocolate mix has nuthin' on this! Make up a big batch and keep it in a mason jar. It's the perfect way to warm up after a fun afternoon playing in the snow!

Ingredients:

  • 2 c powdered sugar
  • 1 c unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 c powdered milk

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients well.
  2. Carefully pour into a quart sized mason jar.
  3. When you're ready for that hot chocolate, add up to 1/8 c of mix in a cup of boiling water.
